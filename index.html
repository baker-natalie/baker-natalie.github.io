White Tiger

White Tigers are not only icredibly fast runners, they are also capable swimmers.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris Tigris
  • Average Length: 3 meters or more
  • Average Lifespan: 10-20 years
  • Habitat: Dense jungles and swamps

Due to a genetic mutation, White Tigers lack the pigment pheomelanin, which gives Bengal tigers their orange color. Like other tigers, the White Tiger's vertical black stripes are unique to the species and individual.
